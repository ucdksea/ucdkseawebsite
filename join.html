How to Join KSEA

Click here to join KSEA

1. Create your account


2. Sign in to your account


3. Go to "My Profile" located in the top right corner


4. Scroll down to find the membership options


5. Click on "Membership Type: Student"


6. Select "Undergraduate" on "School Type", fill in "School Name", and click "Join Free Membership"


7. Click on "KSEA Information" and scroll down


8. Set "Local Chapter" to Sacramento and fill out "Technical Group" and "Specialty"


9. If your membership card looks like the example below, you are all set!
